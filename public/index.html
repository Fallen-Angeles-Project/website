Cafesjian Center for the Arts - Yerevan - Fall 2024 Show

May 6, 2024 DRAFT
Fallen Angels Monument - Coming in Fall 2024 Layering Reason - is a monument by artist Zadik Zadikian as a dedication from the Armenian community to the fallen soldiers of the 2020 war in Nagorno-Karabakh that took the lives of thousands of Armenian soldiers. For Armenians around the globe, this is a tragic topic. The goal of this monument is to immortalize the fallen soldiers and give the viewer a visceral experience of the scale of loss inflicted by the conflict.…
