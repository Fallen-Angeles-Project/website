Support Our Mission

Fallen Angels Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to elevating Armenian culture on the global stage through the power of art. Your tax-deductible donation helps us support major international projects, including the Armenian Pavilion at the Venice Biennale 2026 and other critical cultural initiatives.

Tax-Deductible Contributions

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, all donations to Fallen Angels Inc. are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law. Our Federal Tax ID (EIN) is available upon request.

How Your Donation Helps

Your support enables us to:

  • Present Armenian artists and culture at major international exhibitions
  • Foster dialogue and cross-cultural exchange through art
  • Support educational programs that deepen awareness of Armenian cultural heritage
  • Champion creative expression as a bridge between communities
  • Advance Armenia’s artistic innovation on the world stage

Ways to Give

For information about making a donation, please contact us at info@fallenangelsproject.org.

We deeply appreciate your support in elevating Armenian culture through art.
